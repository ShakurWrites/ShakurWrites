How Do I Say I Love You

How do I say I love you, without saying I love you?
Hey you, I’m sorry—
Hey again, I’m drowning.
Thoughts of you consume me,
pulling me under.
All I’d give to find the safety
of the shore,
but how can I swim away
from the one thing keeping me alive?
So I let it sink me—
An anchor tied to my feet.
How do I say I love you,
Without saying I love you?

“Never meet your heroes,” they say.
But I met you,
And now I’m stuck,
A moth to a flame,
A halo that weighs a ton.
You make my knees weak,
But for you, I’d run a thousand miles,
Barefoot through broken glass,
If it meant being by your side.

I want to say I love you, without saying I love you.
How can something so beautiful
Feel like it’s tearing me apart?
Maybe I’m a coward—
Maybe I’m scared
It might actually work.
But I’d rather have some of you
Than none of you,
Even if it means breaking myself
To keep you whole.

— Abdishakur Mohammed
